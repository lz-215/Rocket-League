Game Overview

Rocket League is a high-powered hybrid of arcade-style soccer and vehicular mayhem with easy-to-understand controls and fluid, physics-driven competition. It's a futuristic sports game where players control rocket-powered vehicles to hit a ball into the opponent's goal.

The game includes casual and competitive online matches, a fully-featured offline season mode, special "Mutators" that let you change the rules entirely, hockey and basketball-inspired game modes, and more than 500 trillion possible cosmetic customization combinations.

Pro Tips & Strategies

Beginner Tips

  • Complete the tutorial to learn the basic controls and mechanics
  • Practice aerial control in free play mode before attempting in matches
  • Use ball cam to keep track of the ball at all times
  • Learn to use powerslide (drift) for quick turns
  • Don't chase the ball - position yourself strategically

Advanced Techniques

  • Master the half-flip for quick direction changes
  • Practice wall shots and air dribbles in training
  • Learn to fast aerial to beat opponents to the ball
  • Use wave dashes to maintain momentum
  • Develop team rotations with consistent teammates
